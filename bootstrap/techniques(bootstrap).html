Pomodoro

Pomodoro is a tool that helps you with your time management. After some time of using this technique, it will be easier for you to deal with your plans on time. On our website you can find 2 options:

  • 25 minutes of work and 5 minutes of break

  • 50 minutes of work and 10 minutes of break

Pareto Principle

The next technique is called the Pareto principle or in simple words 20% of the effort gives 80% of the result. That is, it is preferable to give one-fifth of your time to difficult tasks. Moreover, this technique helps not only in studies, but also in everyday affairs, which can also please with the result.

The Feynman Technique

The Feynman's technique can be applied in any area for testing your understanding and challenging your assumptions. The easiest way to understand concept is explain it to someone else because key is simplicity!

  1. Take a piece of paper and write the concept's name at the title
  2. Explain the concept using simple language and work with examples
  3. Identify problem areas, then go back to the sources to review
  4. Pinpoint any complicated terms and challenge yourself to simplify them
